About us

Our Company

Alfa Associates offers state-of-the art MEP consultancy services for a diverse range of clients in the public sector, private sector, Government departments, Trusts and educational institutions. Alfa Associates is a quality oriented organisation with sound technical back up for Design consultancy services.

Alfa Associates has completed variety of projects in the areas of Healthcare, Hospitality industry, Housing, Public and Commercial buildings, Education and designing Townships among others.

Alfa Associates has made its mark in offering solutions by its commitment to quality following stringent quality standards supported by its highly skilled workforce with valuable knowledge in the technical realm. The services provided by Alfa Associates are value-based, efficient and cost-effective.

Alfa Associates was started in the year 1985 as an Engineering consultancy and construction company to support M/S Bharat Heavy Electricals Ltd and its ancillary units at Trichy in Tamil Nadu. Alfa Associates also has its branch offices at Chennai, Pondicherry & Coimbatore.

Our Strength

Alfa Associates is having many teams of talented design Engineers headed by experienced Professionals in various Engineering discipline to cater the needs of their esteemed customers.

Continuous in-house and external training programs are imparted on each and every Engineer to upkeep their knowledge. All the drawings and documents are prepared by using the latest software’s in the respective Engineering field and the software’s are validated at regular intervals.

Various kinds of standards and codes of practices are followed in every design very strictly according to the nature of the building. Able administrative and accounting staffs are supporting the above technical teams to deliver timely, uncompromised total design solutions.
