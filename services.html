    • Streamlined processes

    Alfa Associates involves itself in every project by meticulously planning and preparing detailed documents and drawings before the execution of a project. These include

  • Ascertaining present condition of site

  • Preparation and submission of design brief report

  • Meeting & discussions with Architect’s / Client / Client’s personnel

  • Obtain approval of concepts by Architect’s / Client / Client’s personnel

  • Preparation of detailed GFC drawings of the project wherever necessary

  • Preparation of bill of quantities with budgetary estimates

  • Preparation of technical specifications

  • Preparation of tender documents

  • Support for Bid evaluation

  • Technical support for tender negotiation

  • Periodical site visit / inspection whenever required and quality verification of materials, work execution and progress
